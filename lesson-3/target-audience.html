Description

The target audience for Snapshot Weather is primarily those living in the Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven areas. It is also those who are traveling to any of these locations and are wanting consistent weather updates. The range of the target audience can be anywhere from a child in elementary school to someone retired living in the area.
