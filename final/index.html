Who We Are

Temple Inn & Suites is your home away from home. We hope to provide memorable and sacred experiences as you travel to one of our four locations. Our newest addition is less than half a mile away from the Seattle Temple.

The Seattle Temple

The Seattle Temple is the 21st constructed and 19th operating temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The temple was announced on November 15, 1975 and was dedicated five years later on November 17, 1980.
